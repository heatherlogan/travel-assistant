**Siem Reap to Phnom Penh**:
- **Bus**: Regular bus services are available and take about 5-7 hours.
- **Flight**: A quick 1-hour flight is available for those short on time.

**Phnom Penh to Sihanoukville**:
- **Bus**: This journey takes about 4-6 hours by bus.
- **Train**: A scenic train ride takes approximately 7 hours.
- **Flight**: A short 45-minute flight is also an option.

**Sihanoukville to Battambang**:
- **Bus**: No direct buses; however, you can take a bus to Phnom Penh and then another to Battambang (total about 10-12 hours).

**Battambang to Siem Reap**:
- **Bus**: This route is easily accessible by bus, taking about 3-4 hours.

### Suggested Cambodia Travel Itinerary ðŸ‡°ðŸ‡­

**Siem Reap & Angkor Wat** (3-4 days)
- Visit the Angkor Archaeological Park, including Angkor Wat, Bayon Temple, and Ta Prohm.

**Phnom Penh** (2-3 days)
- Explore the Royal Palace, Silver Pagoda, and the poignant Tuol Sleng Genocide Museum.

**Sihanoukville & Islands** (2-3 days)
- Relax on the beaches or visit nearby islands like Koh Rong or Koh Rong Samloem.

**Battambang** (1-2 days)
- Enjoy the Bamboo Train ride and explore the colonial architecture and local markets.

This itinerary offers a balanced mix of cultural, historical, and natural attractions, ensuring a comprehensive experience of Cambodia's treasures.

**Additional Tips:**
- Consider booking your buses through reputable companies that cater to tourists for safety and comfort.
- Flights are quick but require travel to and from airports, which can add to your journey time.
- Trains provide a more scenic route but may have limited schedules.

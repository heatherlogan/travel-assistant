**Week 1: Northern Vietnam**

- **Hanoi**
  - Stay: Hanoi's Old Quarter Hostel
  - Activities:
    - Explore Hoan Kiem Lake
    - Visit the Temple of Literature
    - Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum
    - Night Market in Old Quarter
  - Dining: Try local street food like Pho and Bun Cha
  
- **Ha Long Bay**
  - Activities:
    - 2-Day Cruise exploring limestone islands and caves
    - Kayaking, swimming, and a sunrise Tai Chi session

**Week 2: Central Vietnam**

- **Phong Nha-Ke Bang National Park**
  - Stay: Easy Tiger Hostel
  - Activities:
    - Explore Phong Nha Cave
    - Dark Cave adventure tour
    - Take a jungle hike

- **Hoi An**
  - Stay: Hoi An Backpackers Hostel
  - Activities:
    - Walk through the Ancient Town
    - Take a Lantern Making Workshop
    - Visit the Japanese Covered Bridge
  - Dining: Local specialties like Cao Lau and Banh Mi

**Week 3: Southern Vietnam**

- **Hue**
  - Activities:
    - Visit the Imperial City
    - Take a motorbike tour to the countryside
    
- **Da Nang**
  - Activities:
    - Explore Marble Mountains
    - Relax at My Khe Beach

- **Ho Chi Minh City (Saigon)**
  - Stay: The Hideout Hostel
  - Activities:
    - Visit Cu Chi Tunnels
    - Explore Ben Thanh Market
    - War Remnants Museum
  - Dining: Enjoy Vietnamese coffee and Banh Xeo

**Additional Tips:**  
- Always carry a rain poncho as weather can be unpredictable.
- Use overnight buses to save on accommodation and cover long distances.
- Engage with locals for unique experiences and off-the-beaten-path recommendations.
